Taiwanese Romanization Writing System

The official romanization system for Taiwanese Hokkien (Taiwanese) in Taiwan is locally referred to as Tâi-uân Bân-lâm-gí Lô-má-jī Phing-im Hong-àn or Taiwan Minnanyu Luomazi Pinyin Fang'an (lit. 'Taiwanese Hokkien Romanization Solution'), often shortened to Tâi-lô. [Wikipedia]
